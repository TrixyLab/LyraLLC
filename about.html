Who We Are

BUILDING A
LEGACY

Lyra is a premier North American esports organization founded on the pursuit of competitive excellence and community engagement.

From Contenders to Champions

Founded in 2021 by Versa, LYRA emerged with a single vision: to build an organization where players are empowered to reach their absolute peak.

Over the last few years, we've grown from a single competitive roster into a global powerhouse, competing across VALORANT, League of Legends, Apex Legends, Halo, and Rocket League. We don't just participate; we set the standard.

At LYRA, we believe that true victory comes from unparalleled dedication, cutting-edge facilities, and an unyielding connection with our fanbase that fuels us on the biggest stages in the world.
